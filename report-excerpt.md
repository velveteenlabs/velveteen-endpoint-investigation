# Report Excerpt

## Executive Summary

A structured investigation identified indicators of unauthorized endpoint activity involving scripted execution, persistence mechanisms, and external communication.

The activity suggests deliberate system interaction consistent with common intrusion techniques.

**Risk Level: HIGH**

---

## Key Indicators

* Scheduled Task: WinReconService
* PowerShell ExecutionPolicy Bypass
* Suspicious DLL files
* Outbound communication over port 443

---

## Conclusion

The correlation of persistence, execution, and communication indicators supports the conclusion of coordinated endpoint activity rather than isolated anomalies.
